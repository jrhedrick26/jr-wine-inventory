# 🍷 JR Wine Cellar - User Guide & Quick Start

Welcome to your private digital wine cellar! This guide will help you navigate the app, log your wines, and get personalized recommendations from your AI Sommelier assistant.

---

## 🔑 1. Signing In
1. Open the application link on your phone or computer.
2. Enter your unique **Personal Access Code** in the sign-in prompt.
   * *Note: Your access code keeps your cellar inventory separate and private from other members.*
3. Click **Sign In** to open your dashboard.

---

## ➕ 2. Logging a New Bottle (Log a Bottle Tab)

You can add wines to your cellar either by taking photos of their labels or entering their details manually.

### A. Scanning Labels (Camera / Photo Upload)
* **Single Image**: Upload or capture a single photo of a wine bottle label. The built-in AI will scan it, extract the winery, varietal (grape), and vintage (year), and automatically fill out the manual form for you to review.
* **Multiple Images (Bulk Upload)**: Upload multiple photos at once. The app will scan all of them and present them in a clean table where you can adjust the quantity of each bottle before saving.

### B. Two Intake Modes
When logging a bottle, you have two choices for where the bottle goes:
1. **📦 Storing it in my cellar**: Adds the wine directly into your **Active Cellar** stock. If you already have that exact wine, it increments your quantity.
2. **🍷 Drinking it right now!**: Bypasses the cellar inventory and logs it directly into your **Cellar History**. It will prompt you for an immediate rating (*Loved*, *Liked*, *Disliked*, or *None*).

---

## 🍷 3. Managing Your Stock (Active Cellar Tab)

This tab displays everything you currently have in stock.

* **Cellar Metrics**: Shows your total active bottle count and the number of unique grape varieties you own.
* **Active Stock Table**: Displays your wines, grouped to prevent duplicates. Select any row to open the **Wine Detail Panel**.
* **Wine Detail Panel**:
  * Displays the **Wine 101 educational profile** (Origin, Tasting Notes, and Pairings).
  * **📋 Share Wine Details**: Generates a text message card containing the wine facts and tasting notes that you can copy to clipboard and text to a friend.
  * **🍷 Bottle Finished**: Click this when you drink a bottle. It will ask for your rating (*Loved*, *Liked*, *Disliked*, or *None*).
    * If you had multiple bottles of this wine, the quantity decrements by 1.
    * If it was your last bottle, it updates your active stock and saves the wine to your history.

---

## 📜 4. Viewing Your Past Drinks (Cellar History Tab)

This tab lists all the wines you have consumed, sorted with your most recent drinks at the top.

* **⭐ My Favorites**: Toggle this option to filter your history and display only bottles you rated as **"Loved"** or **"Liked"**. This is a great shopping list for your next trip to the wine store!
* **🔄 Restore Bottle**: Select a consumed wine in the history list and click **Restore Bottle to Active Cellar** to move it back to your active stock (its rating will reset to "None").

---

## 💬 5. Chatting with Your Sommelier (Cellar Chat Tab)

The Sommelier assistant is an AI chatbot that knows exactly what you have in stock and how you rated past bottles. 

### What You Can Ask:
* *"What wine from my cellar goes best with grilled salmon?"*
* *"Recommend a bottle from my stock to drink tonight."*
* *"Based on the wines I loved in my history, what style of wine should I buy next?"*
* *"Tell me more about the Chardonnay grape."*

### Recommendation Priority:
1. The assistant will always suggest matching bottles from your **Active Cellar** first.
2. If nothing matches, it will suggest styles or grape varieties similar to past bottles you rated as **"Loved"** or **"Liked"** in your history.
3. If your cellar is empty, it will suggest bottles to purchase retail.
